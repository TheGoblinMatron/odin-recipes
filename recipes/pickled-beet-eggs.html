Pickled Beet Eggs

Description

Pickled beet eggs are a tradition from the Pennsylvania Dutch.

There are lots of variations, this one is my family's! You'll need a large glass jar!

Ingredients

  • 8 eggs, hard boiled & peeled
  • 2 (15 oz) cans pickled beets
  • 1 small onion sliced
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 3/4 cup white vinegar
  • 12 whole cloves
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Open beets, strain over pot on stove. Set beets aside for later.
  2. Mix sugar, vinegar, cloves, cinnamon, and salt into beet liquid. Simmer for 10 minutes to blend flavors.
  3. Into your large jar, layer beets, onions and eggs so they're evenly distributed.
  4. When liquid has simmered, pour into jar with eggs.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 48 hours.
  6. Slice and enjoy!
